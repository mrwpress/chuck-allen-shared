## The Rebrand
Chuck is pivoting from WordPress-focused (MR. WPress) to a broader modern web development brand under his own name. Cross-pollination strategy:
- "A ChuckAllen.ai Company" branding on mrwpress.com
- Leverage existing MR. WPress reputation to bootstrap the new brand
- Former non-WordPress clients writing starter reviews

## Target Audience
**Developers and agencies** — NOT end clients looking for basic builds. People who need:
- Senior technical leadership (manage, lead, see projects through)
- Complex/non-mundane builds requiring deep technical skill
- A reliable technical partner, not just a contractor

## Technical Focus
**Frameworks:** Astro, Next.js, Vue, Node, React, modern JS frameworks
**Specialty areas (passion):**
- Data visualization & reporting
- Automation, alerts & notifications
- Rapid deployment
- Scalable architecture & serverless design

**API Integration Experience:**
- Google APIs
- ShipStation
- Outscraper
- Payment gateways
- Many others

## Site Purposes
- **chuckallen.ai** — Umbrella company brand / modern technical consultancy. NOT an "AI company." AI and automation are tools in the toolkit, not the identity. Avoid hype language. Positioned as: "Custom web development, automation, data systems, and API integrations — with AI where it actually makes sense."
- **chuckallen.dev** — Personal developer brand. The "hire me" site targeting agencies and dev teams needing senior technical leadership.

## Working Model
- **White label** — invisible to the agency's clients
- **Billing:** Hourly, but per-project negotiable
- **Availability:** US timezone (Mountain/Colorado), available for calls, text, screen share during business hours
- **18 years** of programming and development experience

## Competitive Positioning (vs Upwork/Fiverr)
- Results-driven, long-haul partner — not a churn-and-burn freelancer
- Invests in understanding the client's vision and seeing it through
- US-based, same timezone, real-time communication
- Enterprise-level planning and execution brought to every project
- The talent problem on gig platforms is real — Chuck is the antidote

## Demo/Portfolio Plan
- Has existing data viz, reporting, and automation work to show
- Will also build demo pieces to flesh out the portfolio

**Why:** Starting from scratch reputation-wise outside WordPress. Needs to establish credibility fast with a technical audience that can evaluate skill quality.

**How to apply:** Everything we build should speak to developers and agencies as peers. No fluff — show technical depth, real integrations, architecture decisions. The sites themselves ARE the portfolio. Copy tone should be direct, confident, peer-to-peer. Anti-Upwork positioning is a key differentiator.
